👨🏻‍💻 I'm a first year PhD student at Georgia Tech.

🔬 My research interests are broadly in the domains of applied machine learning and sensor data analysis. In particular, I work in the areas of self-supervised learning, representation learning, and domain adaptation, with a focus on Human Activity Recognition!  

📚 I completed my master's degree in Computer Science (ML Specialization) from Georgia Tech.

🥇 Before joining Georgia Tech, I pursued my bachelor's degree from IIIT Guwahati, where I received President's Gold Medal with University Rank 1.
